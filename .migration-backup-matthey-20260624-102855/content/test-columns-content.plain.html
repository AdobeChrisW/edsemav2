Our everyday dependence on PGMs

PGMs are the hidden superheroes powering technology all around us; from the phones in your pocket, our ever-growing thirst for data, and day-to-day transportation, to life-changing medicines, medical devices such as pacemakers, and the clean air that we breathe.

Their unique properties mean they cannot be replaced in many high-performance applications.

The PGM opportunity

Although these metals are geologically rare and mined in small quantities, they are far more accessible than you may think.

After decades of industrial use - especially in catalytic converters - PGMs are already circulating in the global economy in substantial quantities. This has created well established supply chains and recycling networks.
